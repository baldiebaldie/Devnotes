Affective computing
01-12-2026

Affective computing: Field developing systems that recognize, interpret, process, and simulate human emotions to create more empathetic and responsive technology
### What is affect?
- Emotions - angry, sad, happy (Something that happened and you reacted. they are quick and intense)
- Moods - depressed, irritable, cheerful (More long term)
- Interpersonal stance - distinct, cold, supportive (effects how you might interact with someone although you might)
- Preferences - Whether you like something, love, hate
- Personality - Nervous, anxious, reckless
- Culture - collectivist

### What is context?
Can be both internal and external
Types of context:
- Mood 
- Memories
- People around you 
- History

 Ways to apply affective computing
- Applications
- healthcare
- education
- defense
- gaming

What modalities are used?
- Camera (RGB)
